C — Both (recommended)

Chapter notes for in-context thoughts. Scratchpad for synthesis, todos, and exam prep across the whole book. AI can draw from both.

My recommendation: C. A chapter note is the natural replacement for "highlight-required" — same context, no friction. The scratchpad is where studying actually happens: connecting ideas, writing exam summaries, tracking questions. AI works best when it has both: it can pull from chapter notes for specifics and from the scratchpad for the student's big-picture thinking.
